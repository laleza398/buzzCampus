About BuzzCampus

Student-developed. Community-driven. Built to amplify campus life.

BuzzCampus was created by a group of South African university students who were tired of fragmented campus experiences — missing events, paying too much, struggling to get home safely at night, and feeling disconnected from support when it mattered most. We built the platform we always needed: one digital hub that brings events, deals, transport, a marketplace and wellness together for every SA student.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

🛡️

Safety

BuzzRide and verified profiles ensure every student can move and connect safely, day and night.

🤝

Community

We connect students across campuses, courses and cultures — because better connections mean better lives.

💰

Affordability

Every feature is designed with NSFAS students and tight budgets in mind. No hidden costs. No exclusion.

🌍

Inclusion

Our Journey

1
April-10-2026 — The Idea

Born from frustration

A group of 10 students at Sol Plaatje University identified the gap: no single platform connected campus life digitally.

2
April-20-2026 — Development

Building the Platform

We spend days and nights designing, testing, and building BuzzCampus with real student feedback from 5 universities.

3
April-30-2026 — Launch

Going Live

BuzzCampus officially launched across 3 universities, onboarding 2,000 students in the first month.

4
May-01-2026 — Growth

Scaling Nationally

Expanding to all 26 SA public universities, adding BuzzRide nationwide and partnering with major student brands.
