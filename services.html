🚖 BuzzRide — Safe Student Transport

BuzzRide connects students with verified, campus-approved drivers for safe late-night and early-morning transport. Integrated with Google Maps for live tracking.

  • Verified drivers — background-checked and campus-approved
  • Live GPS tracking shared with trusted contacts
  • SOS button instantly alerts campus security and SAPS
  • Female-only ride option available at all times
  • Affordable rates — from R15 for on-campus routes
  • Scheduled rides for early morning exam slots
